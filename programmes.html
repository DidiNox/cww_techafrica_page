Programmes

CTA Cohort programme

This program is open to all youths in Africa between the age of 18-35.
The program will equip the youths with Tech skills, soft skills and entrepreneurship training. It's scheduled to hold for 6 weeks virtually. It is an instructor led training with practical classes and real life projects.
Every trainee would have access to a mentor to guide them.

Application for CTA Cohort 7.0 is ongoing! Apply now

CTA Bootcamp programme

The bootcamp programme is a 4 weeks intensive and innovative programme that offers you access to build real-life projects, increase your portfolio
collaborate with cross functional teams, professional project review, and mentorship from industry experts.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long is CWW Tech Africa program?

    The duration depends on the program. We currently have 3 cohort-based programmes.

    • CTA Cohort programmes -Duration of program is 6 weeks
    • CTA Boootcamp – 4 weeks
    • CTA Soft skills training – 2 days

  • Is the program remote?

    CWW Tech Africa programs are virtual training programs that allow all participants, regardless of location, to actively participate

  • How do I join a CWW Tech Africa program?

    To join our programs, click on "Apply as a trainee" on any of our programmes at cww.techafrica.com

  • When will I be notified about the results of my application?

    CWW Tech Africa will contact all applicants through email in due course. All successful applicants will be notified

  • Are CWW Tech Africa programmes open to undergraduates?

    Yes, we encourage all students to apply
